A long history of care.

Lawn Love has been caring for lawns for over 60 years. The tools we use today might have changed, but our commitment to quality hasn't budged since we cut our first blade of grass in 1956.

We're proud to be locally owned and operated. It allows us to hire familiar faces that you can trust with your lawn.

Bill Glaston

Bill is the founder of Lawn Love. He started Lawn Love back in 1956 with one small lawn mower and a couple neighborhood yards. Today Bill can still be found sharpening blades and oiling shears at Lawn Love HQ.

Shay Bernard

Shay is the head designer at Lawn Love. With over a decade of experience, Shay has worked on projects for Fortune 500 companies and families of 5. He takes pride in finding maintainable solutions regardless the size of the client or property.

Garrett Martin

Odds are you've seen Garrett around town. Garrett has been with Lawn Love for over 10 years and specializes in landscaping design.
